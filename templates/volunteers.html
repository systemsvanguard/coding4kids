Volunteers

We strive to keep a 4:1 student to coach ratio to ensure the children gain the most interaction and feedback in the courses. We provide positive role models for our students. In addition to expert hired staff, we actively seek dedicated volunteers with a love for seeing children learn. Our coaches find it a very rewarding experience in seeing the light come on in children's eyes as they suddenly grasp a complex topic. Or maybe its just seeing kids moving towards their potential. If this sounds like you, we would love to hear from you. Below are current volunteer vacancies.
